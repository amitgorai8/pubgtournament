Rules

  • The tournament is being organized by Insane Gaming.
  • This tournament will consist of 30 squads of 4 players each.
  • An entry fees of Rs. 40 per squad will be charged.*
  • Team leader will have to upload the screenshot of the payment made at the respective placed in the registration form
  • These 30 squads will be further divided into 2 groups of 15 squads each.
  • These 15 squads will compete in their respective maps i.e.

    • Group 1 of 15 squads will play in Erangel.
    • Group 2 of 15 squads will play in Sanhok.

    • Groups will be allotted randomly.
    • Once allotted, any request regarding change of group will not be entertained.
    • Top 7 squads from each group will qualify for the finals.
    • Final qualifying squads will compete in 2 maps(SANHOK and ERANGEL)
    • Final score after the completion of these 2 matches will decide the winner of the tournament and rewards will be distributed.

      • 1st Position will get Rs. 500
      • 2nd Position will get Rs. 300
      • 3rd Position will get Rs. 100
      • The Squad with highest kills will get Rs. 100

    • NO emulator is allowed. The tournament is strictly restricted for the mobile players.
    • If anyone found with any kind of malicious activity, the whole squad will be disqualified.
